What is a Casebook?

A place where all your saved judgments are stored and indexed.

Your Casebook enables you to store important judgments so that you don’t have to reinvent the wheel of your research ever again. Saving and storing will never be easier. All you have to do is click on the ‘Save’ button at the bottom of every case summary in order to start.
